About Us

We work with no range limit

We use the Sigfox protocol to communicate with our devices.
So we can locate your bike everywhere, anytime.
If your bike gets stolen, you can transmit the exact position to the local authorities.

It's a fast and reliable way to find your bike.

The product is easy to install and comes in two forms.
First, The Stick, hide it anywhere you want, it's small and lightweight.
The second product is a stem with a built-in tracker.
